Responsibilities of an MS CCC-SLP

  • Provides skilled SLP evaluation and therapy services for adult patients with speech, language, voice, cognitive, and swallowing disorders within short term rehab and long term care settings.
  • Administers standardized assessments and developes patient-centered plans of care.
  • Educates, counseles, and collaborates with patients, family members, and medical professionals during team meetings and within one-on-one settings.
  • Manages a caseload between 6 to 12 patients.
  • Completes initial evaluations, screenings, daily treatment encounter notes, progress reports, recertification summaries, discharge summaries, g-codes, and associated billing.
